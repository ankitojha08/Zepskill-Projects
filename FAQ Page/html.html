FAQ

Who is eligible to apply for the job guarantee course?

Final year students and past graduates of any degree can apply for our program. We accept students from diverse backgrounds and ensure 100% placement for everyone who is selected.

Is there any upfront fees or registration fees?

No. We don't charge any upfront fee from our students. Our course is entirely pay after placement.

Is there a selection process for this course?

Yes. We have limited number of seats, so we need to select only the most deserving students. But our selection process is not based on prior experience. We accept all hardworking students irrespective of their prior experience with coding.

When do I have to pay the course fee?

The course fee needs to be paid only when you land a job paying 5 or 7 LPA or more. You can pay it in easy instalments when you earn your salary.
